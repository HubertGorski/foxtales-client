Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-01

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected when using the Foxtales application, how it is used, who it may be shared with, and what rights you have regarding the processing of your personal data.


1. Data Controller

The controller of your personal data is:
Hubert Górski
Contact: office @ foxtales.cc

The Controller processes data in accordance with the applicable laws, including the GDPR.


2. Data Collected

The application may collect the following data:

  • email address provided during registration,
  • username,
  • technical data automatically transmitted by your browser or device, such as:
    • IP address,
    • browser type and version,
    • device and operating system information,
    • server logs and traffic statistics (Cloudflare).

The application does not collect sensitive data (such as health, political, or biometric data).


Technical data comes from the communication between your browser or device and the application, as well as from security and infrastructure services provided by Cloudflare.


All personal data is obtained directly from the user or automatically from the device when using the application. The application does not obtain data from third parties.


3. Purposes of Data Processing

Your data is processed for the purpose of:

  • ensuring proper functioning of the application and user accounts,
  • maintaining and managing accounts,
  • ensuring security, detecting and preventing misuse,
  • traffic analysis and technical statistics to improve the application,
  • handling user inquiries and support requests.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Data is processed on the basis of:

  • Article 6(1)(b) GDPR — processing necessary for the performance of a contract or actions prior to entering into a contract (e.g., account maintenance),
  • Article 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interest of the Controller (ensuring system security, traffic analysis, preventing misuse),
  • Article 6(1)(a) GDPR — user's consent, if required for additional application features,
  • Article 6(1)(c) GDPR — compliance with legal obligations (e.g., system security).

Technical data such as IP address, server logs, and browser or device information is processed on the basis of the Controller’s legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) to ensure security, detect misuse, and maintain the stability of the application.


Providing personal data is voluntary, but some data — such as email address and username — is necessary to register and use the application. Without it, creating an account is not possible.


5. Data Sharing and Processors

Your data is not sold. It may only be shared with trusted entities processing data on behalf of the Controller:

  • Cloudflare — CDN, security, traffic analysis,
  • OVH — hosting,
  • Zoho — email services.

Data recipients may also include IT service providers, hosting providers, security companies, and other entities operating under a data processing agreement.

Regarding misuse prevention, Cloudflare may act as an independent data controller.


6. Data Transfer Outside the European Economic Area (EEA)

Some services (such as Cloudflare) may process data on servers outside the EEA. Appropriate safeguards are applied, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) .


7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The application uses only cookies that are necessary for operation (e.g., session cookies). We do not use marketing or tracking cookies. You may manage cookies in your browser settings.


8. Data Retention Period

Data is stored for the following periods:

  • account data — until the account is deleted + backups for up to 90 days,
  • server logs — 90 days,
  • consent-based data — until consent is withdrawn.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • access your data,
  • rectify your data,
  • erase your data,
  • restrict processing,
  • data portability,
  • object to processing,
  • withdraw consent at any time.

10. Data Deletion Procedure

To delete your account, send a request to office @ foxtales.cc including the email address of the account.


11. No Profiling

The Controller does not use profiling or automated decision-making within the meaning of Article 22 GDPR.


12. Data Security

We apply technical and organizational measures to ensure data security, such as encrypted transmission, server protections, and access control.


13. External Links

The application may contain links to third-party websites. The Controller is not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.


14. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes. Significant changes will be communicated by updating the date at the top of this document.
