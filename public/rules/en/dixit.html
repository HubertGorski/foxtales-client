How to play?

  1. The game draws a player who is the subject of the question.
  2. Everyone writes their answer.
  3. The chosen player picks one answer they like the most. The other players choose which answer they think belongs to the chosen player.

Scoring

  • The chosen player gets 10 pts for everyone who voted for their answer (but 0 pts if everyone guessed it).
  • Everyone who voted for the chosen player's answer receives 10 pts.
  • The player whose answer was picked by the chosen player receives an additional 10 pts

Goals

  • When you are the chosen player you want many people to guess your answer‚Ä¶ but not everyone. Your goal is to answer in a way that is not obvious to everyone.
  • When you are not the chosen player you want as many people as possible to choose your answer. That way the chosen player receives fewer points.

    • When you are not the chosen player, you only receive points for guessing the chosen player's answer!

    + Bonus if the chosen player picks your answer
