How to play?

  1. The game draws a player who is the subject of the question.
  2. The chosen player writes their answer truthfully. The other players try to write the same answer as the chosen player.
  3. The chosen player selects the players' answers that they consider correct.

Scoring

  • The chosen player gets 10 pts if at least one person wrote a correct answer.
  • Everyone who wrote a correct answer receives 10 pts.

Team Variant

  • The chosen player gets 10 pts if all members of their team wrote correct answers.
  • A player from the chosen player's team gets 10 pts if they and the rest of their team wrote correct answers.
    Otherwise, all team members receive 0 pts.
  • A player outside the chosen player's team gets 10 pts if they wrote a correct answer - regardless of whether the other members of their team wrote correct answers.

When you are not the chosen player, try to write the same answer as they do.
